Beyond the job, Graphic Design answers an existential requirement for people. This is the solution to human problems and concerns.

How to find where the toilets are? How to transmit values through signs, forms, symbols or colors? How to communicate about an activity, a place or an event? What to do? How to do that?

A lot of questions that graphic design tries to answer. Graphic design is solving problems to help people while making choices.

With graphic design we talk about all.

For MAPLE STUDIO, this is a place where everything needs to be built.

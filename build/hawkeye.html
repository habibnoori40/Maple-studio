HAWKEYE VINTAGE

FEBRUARY 2023

MELBOURNE VIC. AUSTRALIA

Hawkeye Vintage is a specialized brand in the shopping
and retailing of luxurious vintage products.

@HAWKEYEVINTAGE
  • 01 - FIRST IDEA / EYE / SYMBOL / FIGURATIVE
  • 02 - LOGOTYPE / MONOGRAM / H + V
  • 03 - RESPONSIVE / ARZIDENZ GROTESK / AUS / VIC
  • 04 - STATIONARY / LETTERHEAD / CARDS
  • 05 - BUSINESS CARD / CONTACT
↓

The meaning of the name hawkeye refers to the hawkeye's eye, a specialist's eye, a sharp eye, an eye for descovring exclusive products.

In the logo design, Maple Studio initially proposed symbols around the eye's idea.

But Hawkeye's team wanted something different: a glyph with a strong, immediatly recognizable image.

And subsequently, the monogram emerged as a meaningful symbol to represent the luxury and seriousness of the brand. immediatly recognizable image.
